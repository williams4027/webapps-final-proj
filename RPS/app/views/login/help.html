Help

RPS Online is an online game that lets you play Rock, Paper, Scissors against people from behind your computer.

To play:

  • Create an account.
  • Either start a new game or choose from the available games in the <%= link_to('Lobby', {:controller => 'game', :action => 'lobby'}) %>.
  • Choose your move (Rock, Paper, or Scissors).
  • See how you did!

Remember: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
You can also view how you rank up against other users as well as player statistics at the <%= link_to('Leaderboard', {:controller => 'login', :action => 'leaders'}) %>!
